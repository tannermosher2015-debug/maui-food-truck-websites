The truck.

Maui's most-talked-about Thai - cooked in a trailer, not a chain kitchen.

What started as one truck near Costco grew into three across the island under chef Tom Sribura - the same hands that landed Thai Mee Up on Food Network's Diners, Drive-Ins & Dives and the top of Maui's ʻAipono awards. Same wok, same heat, three places to find it.
