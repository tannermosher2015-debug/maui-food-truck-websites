Our story

"Ice cream has always brought out the best memories of celebration for us — we wanted to bring that warmth to everyone too."

We're the Chong family — a Maui-made, family-owned ice cream company. It started with nostalgic flavors we couldn't stop making, and grew into a mobile cart we bring straight to your celebration — handcrafted, small-batch, and scooped right in front of your guests.

Every batch is made fresh. Every cup can carry your names. That's the whole idea.
