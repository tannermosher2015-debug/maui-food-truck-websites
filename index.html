Our story

Born on Maui, served with aloha

Pā'ina started with a beat-up truck, a family poke recipe, and a simple idea: real island food shouldn't be hard to find. Kainoa fishes the morning catch; Leilani runs the kitchen.

“We cook the food we grew up on — and Maui keeps showing up for it.”

Today you'll find us rotating across the island, feeding locals and visitors the plates that taste like home.

— Kainoa & Leilani
