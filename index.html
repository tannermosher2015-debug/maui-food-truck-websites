The Problem

Social media isn't a website

Instagram is great for photos. It's terrible at answering the three questions that actually sell plates: where are you, when are you open, and how do I book you?

01

They can't find you

Posting "we're at the usual spot 🌮" buries your location in a feed. Visitors searching "food trucks near me" never see it — and drive past.

02

Catering leads vanish

A wedding or office lunch is worth hundreds. If booking means DM'ing you and hoping, most of those people just hire the truck with a real "Request catering" button.

03

You don't own the algorithm

Reach changes overnight and the platform owns your audience. Your own site shows up in Google, works 24/7, and is yours for good.
